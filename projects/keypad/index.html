Product Design
KeyPad is a macro keyboard designed to enhance your productivity by giving you the ability to assign custom macro actions to all 24 keys and the two rotary encoders.

Great look - great feel

We use Cherry MX Blue Switches equipped with our custom blank keycaps. Mark important keys with raised special keys and customize your layout.

All the space you need

- Yet still compact:

Total width: 200mm
Total depth: 60mm

Key size: 20x20mm

Process

This project started among friends as a development attempt of a possible real product. We all wanted a sleek and minimalist macro keyboard with high-quality mechanical switches, so I started with the exterior product design, while the others focused on the PCB design and the controller / configurator software. The design started in Blender for form studies and the concept renders seen here. KeyPad is still in active development and will soon see a first prototype.
